# 🎓 CS Bachelor in Morocco — Directory

An interactive directory of **Computer Science bachelor-level programs** offered across Moroccan public universities, browsable on an interactive map.

🔗 **[Live site](https://itsk1m0.github.io/CS-Bachelors/)**

---

## What's in it

Every institution in the dataset includes:

- **Établissement** — the school name (e.g. Faculté des Sciences et Techniques à Tanger)
- **Université** — the parent university (e.g. Université Abdelmalek Essaâdi)
- **Ville & Région** — city and administrative region
- **Type de formation** — Formation Initiale (FI) or Formation Continue (FC)
- **Filieres** — the specific programmes offered, each tagged with its licence type and fees (when applicable)

> **First card example:** *Faculté des Sciences et Techniques à Tanger* — part of Université Abdelmalek Essaâdi, located in Tanger (Tanger-Tétouan-Al Hoceïma region). Offers two LST tracks: *Ingénierie de développement d'applications informatiques* and *Ingénierie des données (ID)*.

---

## Filters

You can filter independently or combine them:

| Filter | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Type de formation** | Formation Initiale (public, free) or Formation Continue (paid, part-time) |
| **Région** | One of Morocco's 12 administrative regions |
| **Type de licence** | Bachelor, LST, LP, LUS, LU, LE, or LNG |

**Type de formation** and **Région** work on their own. **Type de licence** requires at least one of the other two filters to be active as well

You can also **click a city bubble** on the map to see only institutions in that city, and click it again to deselect.

## Data

Data was collected manually from official Moroccan university websites. It covers CS-related bachelor programmes (Informatique, Génie Logiciel, Réseaux, IA, Data Science, etc.) across all regions. Some institutions may have empty filière lists where programme details weren't publicly available at time of collection.

> **Note:** Some school websites only support HTTP (not HTTPS). If a site link doesn't open, try removing the `s` from `https://` in your browser's address bar.
